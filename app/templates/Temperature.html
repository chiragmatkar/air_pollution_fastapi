Temperature

Extreme changes in temperature increase mortality [1]. They also speed up the rate of some chemical reactions, user’s heart rate data stream during their sleep. During the night, ambient temperature has an effect on a user’s heart rate while sleeping. 

Temperature effect on Resting Heartrate:

This data was collected for one user over 424 nights of sleep using the iPhone mobile device with Sleep Cycle Application and Garmin Fenix 5 Watch. We clearly see increases in ambient temperature causes increases the user’s circulation flow (reflected by resting heart rate). Similar analyses can be performed to find the effect of other atmospheric streams on the user’s observed health and behavioral outcomes.
